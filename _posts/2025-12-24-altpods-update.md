AltPods `v1.55.0` were released to Maven central.  
New scan performed to sync with recent releases and available for testing as v1.56.0-SNAPSHOT.

### Updated pods (ones with API changed)

- [Fyber](https://github.com/dkimitsa/robovm-robopods/tree/dev/v1.56.0/fyber)           `v8.4.1` to `v8.4.2`
- [Google Mobile Ads](https://github.com/dkimitsa/robovm-robopods/tree/dev/v1.56.0/google-mobile-ads/ios-google-mobile-ads) `v12.12.0` to `v12.14.0`
- [InMobi](https://github.com/dkimitsa/robovm-robopods/tree/dev/v1.56.0/inmobi/)        `v11.0.0` to `v11.1.0`
- [IronSource](https://github.com/dkimitsa/robovm-robopods/tree/dev/v1.56.0/ironsource/)   `v9.0.0` to `v9.2.0`
- [HelpShift](https://github.com/dkimitsa/robovm-robopods/dev/v1.56.0/alt/helpshift/ios) `v10.4.1` to `v10.5.0`
- [Singular](https://github.com/dkimitsa/robovm-robopods/tree/dev/v1.56.0/singular/)   `v12.9.0` to `v12.9.2`
